01 / About

A student shaped
by water.

Born in 2005, raised on the French Atlantic coast, now designing vessels in Southampton — and still returning every summer to teach sailing at home.

Second‑year student in Yacht & Powercraft Design and Engineering at Solent University. I design only what can be built, and only what behaves well at sea.

My approach is simple. Five consecutive summers as a certified sailing instructor at Terenez FFVoile gave me an intuitive reading of vessels from the user's side — before any CAD file is opened. That grounding now shapes every hull line and structural choice I make.

From composite hull fabrication at Chantier Naval Miossec to parametric hull development in Maxsurf, structural scantlings per ISO 12215, HVAC sizing and propulsion calculations — I bring technical rigour and a practical sense of the water to every project.

Beyond the desk: club rugby since age eleven, scrum‑half for the senior team in Southampton, and a small collection of passports stamped in Japan, Canada, the United States and Indonesia.
